Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes — cancel anytime from your account dashboard with no questions asked. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.
Is there a free trial? +
The Stride Club comes with a 30-day free trial. Full memberships (Monthly and Annual) don't have a trial period, but you can cancel within 7 days for a full refund if BetterBody isn't the right fit.
What's the difference between Monthly and Annual? +
Both give you full access to all programs, challenges, and nutrition content. Annual saves you 17% (equivalent to 2 free months), includes the Stride Club at no extra cost, and unlocks early access to new programs and priority support.
Can I switch plans later? +
Absolutely. You can upgrade from Monthly to Annual at any time from your account settings and we'll prorate the difference. Downgrading takes effect at your next billing cycle.
How does the Stride Club step tracker work? +
When you're about to walk, tap "Start Walk" in your BetterBody dashboard. Your steps are counted in real-time. Tap "Stop Walk" when done — your steps are saved to your profile and added to the weekly leaderboard automatically.
What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), as well as Apple Pay and Google Pay. Payments are processed securely via Stripe.
