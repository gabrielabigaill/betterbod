My Story

Where It All Started

Like most people, I spent years jumping between programs that promised results but never lasted. The workouts were too intense, the meal plans too restrictive, and there was no community to keep me accountable when life got hard. I knew I wasn't alone.

After becoming a certified personal trainer and studying nutrition and wellness, I started coaching clients one-on-one. I saw the same pattern everywhere: people didn't lack discipline — they lacked a system that actually fit their life.

"Transformation isn't about being perfect. It's about showing up — consistently, kindly, and with the right support around you."

— Judith, Founder of BetterBody

BetterBody was born from that insight. I wanted to build a platform where workouts are structured but flexible, nutrition is science-backed but never restrictive, and community isn't just a buzzword — it's the engine. The Stride Club came from my own walking habit: the simplest, most underrated form of movement that changed everything for me and my clients.

Today, BetterBody is home to thousands of members who are moving better, eating better, and living better — not because they're grinding harder, but because they finally have a system built for real people.

What We Stand For

The BetterBody Philosophy

🌱

Sustainable Over Extreme

We reject crash diets and punishing workouts. Every program at BetterBody is designed to build habits you can maintain for life — not just for 6 weeks.

🤝

Community First

The science is clear: people who move and eat well together, stay consistent longer. Community isn't a feature — it's foundational to how BetterBody works.

🧪

Evidence-Based Everything

Every program, recipe, and piece of nutrition guidance is grounded in research. Our dietitian collaborates on all nutritional content — no pseudoscience, ever.

💛

Kindness Toward Yourself

Transformation starts with how you speak to yourself. We build a culture of encouragement, not guilt. Progress over perfection — always.

🔓

Fitness for Every Body

Our programs span all levels, abilities, and schedules. Whether you have 25 minutes at home or an hour at the gym — there's a path for you here.

🚶‍♀️

Movement in All Forms

From heavy lifting to daily walking — all movement counts. The Stride Club exists because walking is one of the most powerful things you can do for your body and mind.

The Method

Move. Nourish. Thrive.

BetterBody is built around three interconnected pillars. When they work together, results become inevitable.

Move — Structured, Progressive Training

Gym programs, home workouts, challenges, and The Stride Club. Clear structure so you always know exactly what to do next.
